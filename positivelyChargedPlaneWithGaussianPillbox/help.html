A Positively Charged Plane With A Gaussian Pillbox

The electric field from the plane passes perpendicularly through the ends of the Gaussian pillbox and does not pass through the sides at all. This makes Gauss's Law easy to work with.

Mouse Interactions
  • Click and drag with the left mouse button to rotate the model.
  • Use the mouse wheel to zoom in and out.
Touch Interactions
  • Swipe with a finger to rotate the model.
  • Pinch with two fingers to zoom in and out.
