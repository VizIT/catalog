Parallel Charged Planes

Two equal and oppositely charged planes generate a strong field between them, but no field whatsoever beyond the planes. We can see this by adding the field from each plane in each region.

Mouse Interactions
  • Click and drag with the left mouse button to rotate the model.
  • Use the mouse wheel to zoom in and out.
Touch Interactions
  • Swipe with a finger to rotate the model.
  • Pinch with two fingers to zoom in and out.
