A Positively Charged Plane

The electric field extends forever along the normal to the plane. We draw only a small part of the plane for practical reasons.

Mouse Interactions
  • Click and drag with the left mouse button to rotate the model.
  • Use the mouse wheel to zoom in and out.
Touch Interactions
  • Swipe with a finger to rotate the model.
  • Pinch with two fingers to zoom in and out.
