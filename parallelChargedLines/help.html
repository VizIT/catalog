Parallel Charged Lines

The electric field tends away from the positive charges in each line. This makes an interesting comparison with the twin positive charges.

Mouse Interactions
  • Click and drag with the left mouse button to rotate the model.
  • Use the mouse wheel to zoom in and out.
Touch Interactions
  • Swipe with a finger to rotate the model.
  • Pinch with two fingers to zoom in and out.
