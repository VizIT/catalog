An Electric Dipole Surrounded By A Gaussian Sphere

Rotate the charges to see the structure of the field. Every field line crosses the gaussian surface zero or two times, giving no net flux through the surface.

Mouse Interactions
  • Click and drag with the left mouse button to rotate the model.
  • Use the mouse wheel to zoom in and out.
Touch Interactions
  • Swipe with a finger to rotate the model.
  • Pinch with two fingers to zoom in and out.
