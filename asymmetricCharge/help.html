An Assymetric Electric Dipole And Its Field

Rotate and zoom in on the charges to see the structure of the field. Because the charges are no longer symmetric, the field lines are no longer closed.

Zoming out shows that at a large distances the field looks more and more like that from a point charge.

Mouse Interactions
  • Click and drag with the left mouse button to rotate the model.
  • Use the mouse wheel to zoom in and out.
Touch Interactions
  • Swipe with a finger to rotate the model.
  • Pinch with two fingers to zoom in and out.
