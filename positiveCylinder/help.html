A Positively Charged Cylinder

The field is very similar to that from a line charge, however there is no electric field in the center of the hollow uniform charge distribution.

Mouse Interactions
  • Click and drag with the left mouse button to rotate the model.
  • Use the mouse wheel to zoom in and out.
Touch Interactions
  • Swipe with a finger to rotate the model.
  • Pinch with two fingers to zoom in and out.
